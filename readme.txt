
  HOW TO PLAY

You control a shape that can switch between two forms:

  CUBE (2D Mode)
    The camera locks to a side-scrolling view. You move left
    and right along a single lane. This mode is faster and
    more responsive, and lets you access orange Cube-only
    platforms that the Sphere cannot use.

  SPHERE (3D Mode)
    The camera becomes free and follows you from behind.
    You can move in all directions, reach blue Sphere-only
    platforms, and access off-axis paths that the Cube cannot
    reach.

The goal is to reach the final checkpoint (flag) at the end
of the Forest zone. Along the way you pass through three zones:

  1. Grassland  - the starting area, learn the basics
  2. Desert     - a moving bridge blocks the path; activate
                  switch to cross
  3. Forest     - elevated wooden platforms, some only
                  reachable in Sphere mode

Reach the last flag to win. Press R to play again from
the menu.


  CONTROLS

  Movement (Cube / 2D)
    A             Move left
    D             Move right

  Movement (Sphere / 3D)
    W             Move forward
    A             Move left
    S             Move backward
    D             Move right
    Mouse         Rotate camera

  Jumping
    SPACE         Jump (hold for a higher jump)

  Mode switching
    1             Switch to Cube (2D)
    2             Switch to Sphere (3D)

  Interaction
    E             Activate a nearby switch

  Other
    R             Respawn at the last checkpoint
    TAB           Toggle fullscreen
    ESC           Quit the game



BRIDGE IN THE DESERT
  The bridge between the Grassland and Desert zones does not
  move by default. Walk up to the green switch near the end
  of the Grassland zone and press E to activate it. The bridge
  will then start sliding back and forth. Time your crossing
  carefully.

SPHERE-ONLY PATHS
  Blue platforms are invisible and impassable while you are
  in Cube mode. Switch to Sphere (press 2) before attempting
  any section with blue platforms. These paths often provide
  shortcuts or reach areas the Cube cannot.

CUBE-ONLY PLATFORMS
  Orange platforms work the same way in reverse. They are
  only solid when you are in Cube mode. Switch back to Cube
  (press 1) to use them.


CHECKPOINTS
  Flags glow red and gold when activated. If you fall off the
  map the game respawns you at the last checkpoint you touched.
  You can also press R at any time to respawn manually.

SWITCHING MODES MID-AIR
  You can switch between Cube and Sphere while airborne.
  Switching to Cube in mid-air locks your Z position instantly,
  which can be useful for lining up a landing on a narrow
  platform.


  TIPS

  - If you cannot reach a platform in one mode, try the other.
  - Hold SPACE after jumping to rise higher before gravity
    takes over.
  - The Cube accelerates and stops faster, making precise
    platforming easier on tight ledges.
  - The Sphere has a slower top speed but full freedom of
    movement, which is necessary for the off-axis platforms
    in the Forest zone.
